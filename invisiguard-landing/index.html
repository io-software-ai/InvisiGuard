Capabilities核心能力

Evidence, not dials.給你證據，而不是一堆看不懂的數字。

Two complementary engines, a measured certificate, and keyed anti-forgery: the parts most tools leave you to stitch together yourself.兩種互補的引擎、一份實測報告，加上金鑰防偽：這些多半是別的工具要你自己拼湊的部分。

Two engines, one API兩種引擎，一組 API

A classic keyed engine for cropping resilience and high-capacity text, and a deep-learning engine for heavy JPEG and resizing. Pick per use case.經典金鑰引擎抗裁切、能嵌入大量文字；深度學習引擎抗重壓縮與縮放。依用途挑選即可。

Measured robustness certificate穩健度實測報告

On embed, we run a real attack battery on your image and show per-attack survival. Not a whitepaper claim, an on-screen result you can act on.嵌入時就對你的圖跑一輪真實攻擊測試，逐項顯示浮水印是否留存。不是白皮書上的說法，而是能據以決策的當場實測。

Keyed & tamper-resistant金鑰防偽，難以竄改

The mark is derived from your secret key, so it can't be read, forged, or cleanly rewritten by anyone without it, unlike unkeyed AI labels.浮水印由你的金鑰產生，沒有金鑰就無法讀取、偽造或無痕竄改，這是無金鑰的 AI 標記做不到的。

Survives the real world撐得過真實通道

Social-platform re-compression, downscaling, and cropping: between the two engines, the marks cover attacks a single method can't.社群平台重壓縮、縮放、裁切：兩種引擎聯手，涵蓋單一方法無法覆蓋的攻擊。

Embed-time self-check嵌入即自我檢查

Every embed immediately re-reads the mark to confirm it's recoverable, so you never ship a watermark that can't be verified.每次嵌入立即回讀確認可還原，絕不會給你一個永遠驗證不出的浮水印。

Carry text or an ID可帶文字或短 ID

Embed up to 92 bytes of text directly in the pixels for offline proof, or a short ID that resolves to a server-side record. Your choice.可直接在像素中嵌入至多 92 bytes 文字供離線自證，或嵌入短 ID 反查伺服器記錄。由你決定。
